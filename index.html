    Our Story

    We Build Safety That
    Never Comes Off

    Founded in Purpose

    Why NAJDA Exists

    The name NAJDA comes from the Arabic word for rescue — and that's exactly what we set out to build: a product that rescues people when it matters most. Our founders witnessed first-hand how inadequate traditional safety equipment was in real maritime emergencies. Flares that failed. Life rings thrown in the wrong direction. No way to share a live position with rescue services.

    We spent three years consulting with coast guard services, offshore sailors, dive instructors, and naval engineers. The result is NAJDA — a family of wearable devices that don't just protect, but actively communicate, monitor, and respond.

    We are a maritime-first company. That means every design decision, every component choice, every test protocol is driven by one question: will this keep someone alive at sea?

    🌊
    Our Values

    What We Stand For

    Reliability Above All

    A safety device that fails in an emergency is worse than none. We test every component to extremes before it gets near water.

    Human-Centered Design

    A device nobody wears protects nobody. Comfort, wearability, and intuitive controls are non-negotiable requirements.

    Radical Transparency

    We publish our test data, our failure rates, and our limitations openly. Trust is built through honesty, not marketing.

    Accessible Safety

    Life-saving technology should not be luxury-priced. We work hard to keep NAJDA devices affordable for working mariners worldwide.
